    <h2>Broadcast schedule</h2>
    <div class="box article">
      <div class="inline-content infographic full">
        <a href="/radionational/on-air/7418728">

</div><p> </p><p> </p><h5>New shows and voices in 2019</h5><p>ABC RN will explore the issues and ideas that matter to Australians in 2019 with new thought-provoking national shows and presenters to stir the mind and spirit across sport, religion, culture, work and life. ABC RN will continue to nurture the intellectual and cultural life of all Australians in 2019 by welcoming new voices and conversations to our extensive schedule of programs and podcasts.</p><p><strong>New to RN in 2019</strong></p><p><a href="/radionational/programs/sporty/" target="_self" title=""><strong>Sporty</strong></a><strong> </strong>(Saturdays 10.30am, replayed Mondays 5.30am)</p><p>RN will return to the playing field with the launch of <strong>Sporty</strong>, a new program for sports enthusiasts and anyone interested in general fitness.</p><p><a href="/radionational/programs/soul-search/" target="_self" title=""><strong>Soul Search</strong></a><strong> </strong>(Sundays 6pm, replayed Thursdays 12pm)</p><p>RN’s new Religion &amp; Ethics program <strong>Soul Search </strong>will explore the growing desire for meaning in our lives, examining what people believe and why. Presenter Dr Meredith Lake will host a national conversation on faith and meaning in Australia. Soul Search will replace The Spirit of Things, following presenter Dr Rachael Kohn’s decision to retire from the ABC.</p><p><a href="/radionational/programs/this-working-life/" target="_self" title=""><strong>This Working Life</strong></a><strong> </strong>(Tuesdays 11.30am, replayed Sundays 8.30pm)</p><p>In 2019, Best Practice will become a broader discussion about the social and cultural aspects of workplaces and how work impacts us under the new title <strong>This Working Life</strong>. The show is for all workplaces and workers – regardless of their rung on the corporate ladder – with coverage of different organisations and business leaders.</p><p><strong>Extended Sunday Extra </strong>(7am Sundays, replayed Mondays 2am)</p><p><a href="/radionational/programs/sundayextra/" target="_self" title=""><strong>Sunday Extra</strong></a> will be extended by 30 minutes to include features and interviews from the ABC’s extensive network of international correspondents, as part of its mix of current affairs, analysis and investigations. </p><p><a href="/radionational/programs/world-docos/" target="_self" title=""><strong>World Docos</strong></a><strong> </strong>(Saturdays 7pm, replayed Tuesdays 1am)</p><p>The world’s best radio documentaries will be showcased in a new program that draws on the archives of the ABC and our broadcast partners. </p><p><a href="/radionational/programs/rn-arts/" target="_self" title=""><strong>The Arts on RN</strong></a><strong> </strong>(Mondays to Fridays 10am)</p><p>In 2019, RN will continue to lead national conversations about arts and culture under a new series of names. <a href="/radionational/programs/the-book-show/" target="_self" title=""><strong>The Book Show</strong></a><strong> </strong>(formerly The Hub on Books) will move to Mondays, with literary interviews, news and analysis. On Tuesdays, <a href="/radionational/programs/the-stage-show/" target="_self" title=""><strong>The Stage Show</strong></a> (formerly The Hub on Stage) will cover theatre, dance, comedy, opera and musicals. On Wednesdays, <a href="/radionational/programs/the-art-show/" target="_self" title=""><strong>The Art Show</strong></a><strong> </strong>(formerly The Hub on Art) will be the “go to” place for issues affecting artists and audiences. On Thursdays,<a href="/radionational/programs/the-screen-show/" target="_self" title=""> <strong>The Screen Show</strong></a><strong> </strong>(formerly The Hub on Screen) will explore the worlds of film and television. <a href="/radionational/programs/the-bookshelf/" target="_self" title=""><strong>The Bookshelf </strong></a>(with Kate Evans and Cassie McCullagh) remains on air at midday on Friday</p><p> </p><p><strong>Returning to RN in 2019</strong></p><p>Listeners will continue to enjoy their favourite RN returning programs and podcasts in 2019. Download and print the new RN schedule as a PDF <a href="/cm/lb/2889742/data/rn-program-guide-data.pdf" target="_self" title=""><strong>here</strong></a>.
